Conor McGregor's return against Max Holloway headlines **UFC 329 on Saturday, July 11, 2026**, and the way you watch it has changed. In the United States there is no longer a hundred-dollar pay-per-view to buy. UFC numbered events now stream on **Paramount+**, included with a subscription. This guide answers every question about how to actually watch the fight: the plan you need, what it costs, whether there is a free way in, start times, devices, and where to find it in every major country. *You can rate how hyped you are for this fight, and every fight on the card, on [Good Fights](https://goodfights.app).*

## How can I watch McGregor vs Holloway 2 in the US?

In the United States, UFC 329 streams **live on Paramount+**. The early prelims, prelims, and main card all run through Paramount+, so a single subscription gets you the entire night, from the first preliminary bout to the McGregor walkout. There is no separate broadcast and no cable PPV order to place.

## Is UFC 329 a pay-per-view? Do I have to buy the fight separately?

No, not in the US. This is the big change. UFC numbered cards like UFC 329 are now **included with a Paramount+ subscription** instead of being sold as a one-time pay-per-view. You do not pay an extra fee on top of your subscription to unlock the main card. That is a major shift from McGregor's previous fights, which routinely carried a PPV price north of $70 on top of whatever you already paid to watch.

## Which Paramount+ plan do I need for UFC 329? Essential or Premium?

Either one works. UFC events are **not locked to the higher tier**, so both Paramount+ Essential and Paramount+ Premium include the full UFC 329 card. You do not need to upgrade to Premium just to watch the fights.

The difference between the plans is everything around the fights, not the fights themselves. Essential has ads and streams up to 1080p. Premium removes most ads, adds your live local CBS station, the full SHOWTIME library, 4K on supported titles, and offline downloads. For watching one live fight night, Essential is all you need.

## How much does Paramount+ cost?

As of early 2026, **Paramount+ Essential is about $8.99 per month** and **Paramount+ Premium is about $13.99 per month**. Both bill monthly or annually, and the annual plan lowers the effective monthly cost if you plan to keep it. Prices can change, so confirm the current rate when you sign up.

Either way, this is the cheapest a major McGregor fight has ever been to watch legally in the US. A single month of Essential at around nine dollars replaces what used to be a subscription plus a PPV fee.

## Is there any way to watch UFC 329 for free?

There is no official free stream of the main card, and Paramount+ discontinued its standard direct free trial in early 2026. That said, there are a couple of legitimate angles worth knowing:

- **Bundles.** Paramount+ is included free with some other services, such as Walmart+, so if you already pay for one of those you may already have access. Check what you are subscribed to before paying again.
- **New-customer and carrier offers.** Paramount+ periodically runs promotional pricing and partner deals (mobile carriers, retailers, annual-plan discounts). It is worth a quick search for a current offer the week of the fight.

Be wary of "free UFC 329 live stream" sites. They are almost always illegal, riddled with malware and pop-ups, and notorious for cutting out right as the main event starts. For a roughly nine-dollar legal option, the risk is not worth it.

## What time does UFC 329 start?

The card is built around US prime time. Expected start times for the broadcast segments:

- **Early prelims:** 5 p.m. ET / 2 p.m. PT
- **Prelims:** 7 p.m. ET / 4 p.m. PT
- **Main card:** 9 p.m. ET / 6 p.m. PT

## What time will McGregor and Holloway actually walk out?

The McGregor vs Holloway main event is the last fight of the night. Headliners typically walk out **two to three hours after the main card begins**, so plan for a ring entrance somewhere around 11:30 p.m. ET or later, depending on how quickly the earlier main-card fights finish. If you only care about the main event, the smart move is to start watching around 11 p.m. ET and not a moment before.

## What devices can I watch Paramount+ on?

Paramount+ runs on basically everything: smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Vizio), streaming sticks and boxes (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast, Google TV), game consoles (PlayStation, Xbox), phones and tablets (iOS and Android), and any web browser at paramountplus.com. To watch on the big screen, the easiest paths are a smart TV app, a streaming stick, or casting from your phone.

## Can I watch UFC 329 on my TV?

Yes. Install the Paramount+ app on your smart TV or a streaming device plugged into it (Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast), sign in, and the live event will be front and center on fight night. If your TV is older, a sub-$40 streaming stick is the simplest fix.

## How do I watch UFC 329 in Canada?

Canada still uses a **pay-per-view model**. The main card is on **Sportsnet+** (subscription from around C$30 per month), with the event itself sold as a pay-per-view priced around C$60. Early prelims air on linear Sportsnet and TSN. So unlike the US, Canadian fans do still pay a separate PPV fee on top of the streaming subscription.

## How do I watch UFC 329 in the UK?

In the United Kingdom, **TNT Sports** carries UFC 329, streamed through discovery+, with replays available on HBO Max. Because of the time difference, the main card starts around **2 a.m. BST on Sunday, July 12**, and the McGregor walkout lands a couple of hours after that. Set an alarm.

## How do I watch UFC 329 in Ireland?

McGregor's home market gets the fight on **TNT Sports and discovery+**, also in the early hours of **Sunday, July 12**. Expect the main-event walkouts somewhere around **4 to 5 a.m. local time**. If you are staying up for the hometown hero, make the coffee strong.

## How do I watch UFC 329 in Australia?

Australia keeps the pay-per-view model too. The main card is a **pay-per-view through Main Event on Kayo Sports and Foxtel** (you do not need an existing Kayo subscription to order it). The prelims stream on Paramount+ and Network 10, with early prelims on Paramount+ and UFC Fight Pass. Because of the time zone the main card lands on Sunday afternoon local time, which is one of the few markets that gets to watch McGregor at a civilized hour.

## How do I watch UFC 329 in Europe?

It varies by country. **DAZN** is the UFC's home across much of the continent, including Germany, Austria, Italy, Spain, and Portugal, and there the event is included with a subscription rather than sold as a separate pay-per-view. In markets without a local rights holder, **UFC Fight Pass** is the worldwide fallback. Check your specific country's listing closer to fight night, since rights shift.

## Can I watch a replay of UFC 329 if I miss it live?

Yes. On Paramount+ in the US, the event is available on demand after it airs, so you can watch the full card or jump to the main event later. Just steer clear of social media and search until you do, because McGregor's return will be spoiled everywhere within minutes. In other markets, replay availability depends on the broadcaster (TNT Sports and HBO Max offer replays in the UK, for example).

## What is the cheapest way to watch McGregor vs Holloway 2?

In the US, the cheapest legal route is **one month of Paramount+ Essential at around $8.99**, which covers the entire card with no PPV add-on. If you do not plan to keep it, subscribe close to fight week and cancel after. Check first whether you already get Paramount+ free through a bundle like Walmart+, in which case it costs you nothing extra.
