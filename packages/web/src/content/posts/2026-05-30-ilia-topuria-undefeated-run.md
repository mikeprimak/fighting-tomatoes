When Ilia Topuria defends the lightweight title against Justin Gaethje at [UFC Freedom 250 on June 14](/blog/ufc-freedom-250-white-house), he does it as something the sport almost never produces: a champion with a perfect record who has made a habit of starching the best fighters of his era. ## Who is Ilia Topuria?

Topuria is a Georgian-Spanish mixed martial artist and the reigning UFC lightweight champion. Nicknamed "El Matador," he fights out of Spain and has built his reputation on two things: a granite chin and one-punch knockout power in both hands. He is one of the rare fighters who is equally comfortable boxing on the inside and grappling, and the combination has made him a nightmare for every division he has entered.

## Is Ilia Topuria really undefeated?

Yes. He carries a **perfect 17-0 professional record** into Freedom 250, and he is the first fighter in UFC history to win titles in two different weight classes while never having lost a fight. In a sport where almost everyone gets beaten eventually, Topuria has not just stayed unbeaten, he has done it while fighting and finishing the very best opponents available.

## How did Topuria win the featherweight title?

He took it from one of the greatest featherweights ever. At **UFC 298 in February 2024**, Topuria knocked out Alexander Volkanovski in the second round to capture the featherweight championship and end Volkanovski's long, dominant reign. It was the moment Topuria went from rising contender to genuine superstar, because Volkanovski had looked nearly untouchable at 145 pounds for years.

## What happened in Topuria vs Max Holloway?

Something nobody had ever done. At **UFC 308 in October 2024**, Topuria defended his featherweight belt against former champion Max Holloway and knocked him out in the third round. That was the **first knockout loss of Holloway's entire career**, against a fighter famous for one of the toughest chins in the sport. Beating Holloway is hard. Knocking him out cold was a statement that Topuria's power is on a different level.

## Why did Topuria move up to lightweight?

After cleaning out the top of featherweight, Topuria **vacated the 145-pound title in February 2025** and moved up to lightweight permanently. The weight cut to featherweight had become brutal, and he wanted to test himself against a deeper, more dangerous division. Vacating a belt you never lost is a bold move, but Topuria has never been short on confidence.

## How did Topuria win the lightweight title?

In ruthless fashion. At **UFC 317 in June 2025**, he faced former lightweight champion Charles Oliveira for the vacant title and knocked him out at **2:27 of the very first round**. With that win he became the first two-division UFC champion to do it while staying undefeated, and he announced himself as the man at the top of one of the sport's glamour divisions.

## Why did Topuria step away from the UFC after winning the belt?

Topuria stepped away from competition for a personal matter following his lightweight title win, which is part of why this Freedom 250 defense is his first since taking the belt. The layoff is the one small question mark around an otherwise spotless run, and Gaethje will be hoping a touch of ring rust is exactly the opening he needs.

## What makes Ilia Topuria so dangerous?

Power and precision in equal measure. Topuria has now knocked out three different all-time greats in Volkanovski, Holloway, and Oliveira, fighters with elite chins and championship pedigree. He sets traps, he is patient, and he punches with bad intentions the instant an opponent makes a mistake. He does not just win, he ends nights, and he does it against the people everyone said could not be finished.

## Can anyone beat Ilia Topuria?

That is the question Justin Gaethje is being paid to answer at Freedom 250. On paper, the blueprint is to drag Topuria into deep, chaotic water, take away his timing, and make him fight for 25 hard minutes. Gaethje's relentless pressure and famous leg kicks are as good a tool for that job as anyone has. But until someone actually does it, Topuria remains undefeated, and betting against him has been a losing proposition for years.
