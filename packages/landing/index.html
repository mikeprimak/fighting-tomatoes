What is Good Fights?

The fight fan's companion app

Good Fights is the only app that lets you track, hype, rate, and discuss fights across every major combat sports promotion — all in one place.

Whether you're a hardcore MMA fan, a boxing purist, or someone who follows bare-knuckle, karate combat, and everything in between — Good Fights has you covered.

  • See every fight card from 14+ promotions
  • Know which fights are worth watching
  • Join a community that lives and breathes combat sports
Features

Everything a fight fan needs

From building hype before fight night to reliving the best bouts — Good Fights puts the entire combat sports world at your fingertips.

Hype Upcoming Fights

Rate your excitement for upcoming bouts from 1-10. See how the community feels about every fight on the card.

Rate Past Fights

Just watched a war? Rate it. Build your fight rating history and help others discover the best fights to watch.

Comment & Discuss

Share your thoughts on any fight — past or upcoming. Talk predictions, breakdowns, and hot takes with the community.

Never Miss a Fight

Get notified when events go live and fights are about to start. Follow your favorite promotions and fighters.

Live Event Tracking

Follow events in real-time as fights finish. See results, community ratings, and reactions as they happen.

Spoiler-Free Mode

Watching fights on delay? Toggle spoiler-free mode to browse without seeing results until you're ready to reveal them.

Build the hype before fight night

How excited are you for the main event? Rate every fight on the card from 1 to 10 and see where the community stands. The most hyped fights rise to the top — so you always know which bouts have the most buzz.

  • Rate your hype 1-10 for any upcoming fight
  • See the community average hype score
  • Comment your predictions and analysis
  • Discover sleeper fights you might have missed

Rate fights. Find the best ones.

Every fight gets a community rating. Just watched a five-round war? Give it a 10. Boring decision? Rate it a 3. Your ratings help everyone discover the fights that are actually worth watching.

  • Star ratings for every completed fight
  • See community average and rating distribution
  • Browse the highest-rated fights of all time
  • Write reviews to share your take

Follow events live, fight by fight

When an event is live, Good Fights tracks every fight in real-time. See results as they come in, rate fights immediately after they end, and see what the community thought — all without leaving the app.

  • Automatic live tracking for all major promotions
  • Fight results update in real-time
  • Rate fights seconds after they end
  • Spoiler-free mode for delayed viewing
