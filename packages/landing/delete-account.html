Good Fights Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Good Fights account, please send an email to the address below. Make sure to send the request from the email address associated with your account.

Send your deletion request to:

contact@goodfights.app

What Gets Deleted

The following personal information will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • Your display name and profile information
  • Your account and all personal data

What Gets Anonymized

To preserve community ratings and discussions, the following content is kept but fully anonymized (no longer linked to you or traceable):

  • Fight ratings you submitted
  • Hype predictions
  • Reviews and comments

This content will display as "Deleted User" and cannot be traced back to your identity.

Processing Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
