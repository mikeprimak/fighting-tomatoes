### Findings worth flagging
- **Oktagon (CSMMA = Unified, eff. Jan 1 2025) is the conservative mirror of UFC:** still BANS
  the 12-6 elbow the US legalized Nov 2024, AND keeps "single hand down ≠ grounded" (UFC reversed
  this Nov 2024). Same base rules, two opposite calls — strong infographic hook.
- **PFL:** standard Unified in-cage; the distinctive is the season points → playoffs → $1M format.
  Classic scoring = win 3 / finish-bonus by round (or 6/5/4 for R1/R2/R3 finish). NOTE: PFL
  revamped the format for 2025 — verify exact current scoring in Phase 2.
- **BKFC:** circular "Squared Circle", 5×2min (7 title), punches only, wraps illegal within 1" of
  knuckle, toe-the-line round starts, 9 classes 107–266+.
- **RAF:** the outlier — freestyle wrestling, no striking, 3×2min, pin/10-pt tech fall ends it.
