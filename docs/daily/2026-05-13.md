3. **Cross-referenced with App Store Connect + Play Console.**
   - iOS: 202 app units Apr 17–May 12; 33-install spike May 10
   - Android (Play Console, 28d): 158 device acquisitions (+236%), 89 first opens (+162%), MAU 145 (+138%), Play rating 4.20
   - Combined: ~360 cross-platform installs in 28 days. Plan target was +500 in 30 days = ~72% to target with days to go.

4. **First analytical mistake.** Initially attributed May 10 spike to fight-night marketing thesis (`UFC 328 = $80 spend = 30 install spike = thesis validated`). Mike corrected: those 30 were almost all legacy fightingtomatoes.com users following their long-standing Sunday-morning post-card habit, now routed through the legacy redirect to the app store. The $80 spend cannot be credited with any clean signal.

5. **Second correction.** ~50 of the Android installs in the window were paid testers Mike hired to clear Google's 20-tester/14-day pre-launch requirement. They install, they don't open. Corrected install-to-open math: 158 raw → 108 organic → 89 first opens / 108 = **82% organic open rate**, not the 56% panic I raised.
