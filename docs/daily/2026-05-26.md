## Editorial: Figueiredo rating-arc blog post

Second editorial blog post (after the Chimaev/Danis one). Charts Deiveson
Figueiredo's entertainment-rating arc across his whole UFC run.

- **Title:** "Can Deiveson Figueiredo Entertain Fans Again Like He Did in 2020?"
- **Slug:** `can-figueiredo-entertain-again` · live at `/blog/can-figueiredo-entertain-again`
- **Data:** queried prod (Render external) for all COMPLETED UFC fights for
  fighter `95453610-a1ba-4d1e-a11f-9e75934f899f`, ordered by event date —
  21 fights, 2017-06-03 (Beltran) → 2026-01-24 (Umar). Plotted `averageRating`.
  - Peak: Moreno I draw, 2020-12-12, **9.36** (64 votes) — highest of career.
  - Career lows: Montel Jackson **3.40** (2025-10) and Umar **3.00** (2026-01).
  - Story arc: climb → 2020 title era peak → gradual leak after Moreno II
    (2021-06-12) → bantamweight plateau (7.3–7.6) → recent cliff.
  - Caveat noted in copy: early fights have tiny vote counts (2–16); rating
    measures *fun*, not skill.

### Graph approach (reusable)
- `marked` passes raw HTML through, so the graph is an **inline `<svg>`** embedded
  directly in the markdown body — sharp, theme-matched (gold `#F5C518` line on
  `#202020` card), no image asset needed for the in-article chart. Verified the
  SVG survives `marked.parse`.
- For the hero/OG share image (SVG doesn't render as an OG card on Reddit/X),

  - Frontmatter `image:` must point at the **PNG**, not the SVG.
