## Decisions locked with Mike

1. **Build order = enrichment/Fan DNA FIRST**, then home above-the-fold, then
   onboarding. The taste insight is the real user payoff and gates the best home
   cards + the rotating identity line. Invest heavily here.
2. **Home above-the-fold = a dashboard**, two zones:
   - *Fixed/pinned (urgency):* hyped-this-weekend (pinned all week), your-followed-
     fighter-is-fighting, **event-today** (morning awareness) + **event-live-now**
     (distinct from a single fight being live).
   - *Rotating/curated:* taste insights (heavy investment), **non-denigrating**
     group comparisons, and pure **group-data** facts ("75% of users love
     back-and-forth brawls").
3. **Greeting identity line held** until enrichment lands — no static "Hot Take
   Artist" as the app's headline (Mike finds it lame; it never varies).
4. **Phase 1 working model:** long-lived integration branch, dev-DB testing, **no
   prod publish until the whole epic is built + tested** and ships as one
   coordinated release. Mike wants hand-holding through this (limited branch
   experience; past cross-branch contamination pain).
