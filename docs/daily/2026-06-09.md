## The pivot, in one line

**From Rotten Tomatoes (the product is the score, about the fight) → Letterboxd (the
product is YOU, about the fan).** Ratings don't disappear; they become *inputs to an
identity* instead of the output. The home screen, onboarding, and Fan DNA all become
"a site about you."

## Decisions locked this session (all in identity-platform.md)

- **Build order:** enrichment/Fan DNA FIRST → home above-the-fold → onboarding. (The
  taste insight is the real payoff and gates the rest.)
- **Home above-the-fold = a dashboard**, two zones: *fixed/pinned urgency* (hyped-
  this-weekend stays all week, your-followed-fighter-is-fighting, event-today morning
  awareness, event-live-now) + *rotating curated* (taste insights, non-denigrating
  group comparisons, human-framed group facts).
- **Working model:** long-lived integration branch, dev-DB testing, **no prod publish
  until one coordinated release** at the end. Main frozen. (Mike wants the
  professional discipline — limited branch experience, past cross-branch pain.)
- **Two taste axes:** fight character (what kinds of *fights* you rate high) + fighter
  character (what kinds of *fighters* you like). Both enriched, aggregated into one
  profile.
- **Fighter-taste sourcing:** aggregate over fighters in your **highly-rated fights +
  hyped + followed** — NOT follows alone (follows are sparse; ratings are dense).
- **Copy rule (load-bearing):** human, non-statistical HEADLINE ("You love wars");
  the number is a small SUBLINE ("you rate wars ~1.5 higher than most fans"). Group
  facts framed humanly ("brawls are what fans love most"), never "75% of users."
- **Variety:** killed the "the room" tic (was used ~125×) with `communityRef.ts`;
  many phrasings per insight + combinatorial specificity + the engine's 30-day
  cooldown so nothing recurs.
- **Visibility:** Fan DNA **private**; opt-in sharing scoped to year-end "Your Year" /
  Wrapped-style cards (not a blanket public profile).
- **Signature:** **rotating insights only for now** — no single "you are X" frozen
  label (deprecates `personalityType.ts`'s "Hot Take Artist"). Reassess later.
- **Vocab:** **pilot → freeze → backfill** (don't bake a bad vocabulary into 25k
  fights).
- **Taste history snapshotting: DROPPED.** Recomputable from existing
  `FightRating.createdAt`; drift is likely low-signal anyway.
- **Spoiler-safety** binds every new identity surface (existing hard rule).
- **Dev DB:** decision **deferred** — start read-only/branch-only build work first.
